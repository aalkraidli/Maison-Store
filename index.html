About & Contact

Our Philosophy

Less, but
better.

We started MAISON in 2018 out of frustration with fast fashion. Every season, the industry produces more — more styles, more waste, more noise. We wanted to do the opposite.

Today, we release two collections per year. Each piece is designed to coexist with everything else in our range, and in your wardrobe. We work exclusively with suppliers who share our commitment to craft and fair labor.

Our studio is in the 10th arrondissement of Paris. Our team is small. Our standards are not.
